BACHELOR OF
MECHANICAL ENGINEERING


Mechanical engineering is a branch of engineering that deals with the design, analysis,
manufacturing, and maintenance of mechanical systems. It is one of the oldest and broadest
engineering disciplines, encompassing a wide range of subjects and applications. Mechanical
engineers use principles of physics, mathematics, and material science to design and create
mechanical devices and systems that serve various purposes.
